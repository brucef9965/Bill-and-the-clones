Bill and the Clones

There was a rich boy named Bill who dreams of becoming a profesioal engineerer and he had accidently cloned himself when made a clone machine with the clones having different personalities from each other. With these clones, he gets the idea of using them to work for him and make him money. But one day, 5 of the clones had enough and they had the idea to try to take over and rule the world. When Bill found out, he and his other clones tries stop them and save the world. Will they succeed or will they fail and watch the world suffer.

Who do you think will win the fight?
